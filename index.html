Best places to visit in Japan

Here is a few places in Japan that I think you would enjoy. Japan has beautiful scenery, wonderful food, and a great atmosphere overall.

Some of the best places to visit.

Tokyo is the largest metropolitian in the world! There is plenty of food, shopping, and scenery.
Osaka is known for its night life and street food. There is also the Osaka Castle and cherry blossoms to check out!
Kyoto is known as the cultural capital of Japan. There are numerous Buddhist temples and gardens.
Kanazawa has beautiful landscapes. It is renowned for its impeccable geisha and samurai districts.
"If I had to eat only in one city for the rest of my life, Tokyo would be it."
-Anthony Bourdain
